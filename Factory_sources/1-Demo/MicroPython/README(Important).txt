[CN]
烧录完ESP32-S3的MicroPython固件后，如果需要再次烧录固件需要按以下步骤操作：
1、按住ESP32-S3设备上的BOOT按键，再给设备上电，或者直接给设备上电，然后按住BOOT按键，再按RESET按 
      键，先松开RESET按键，再松开BOOT按键。

2、重新选择串口号进行烧录。

注意：
如果已经烧录了ESP32-S3的MicroPython固件，再想烧录其他平台（例如Arduino、ESP32-IDF）程序，按上述步骤操作。

[EN]
After burning the MicroPython firmware of ESP32-S3, if you need to burn the firmware again, you need to follow the steps below:
1. Press and hold the BOOT button on the ESP32-S3 device, then power on the device, or power on the     
    device directly, then press and hold the BOOT button, then press the RESET button, release the RESET   
    button first, and then release the BOOT button.

2. Re select the serial port number for burning.

be careful:
If you have already burned the MicroPython firmware for ESP32-S3 and want to burn programs for other platforms (such as Arduino, ESP32-IDF), follow the above steps.
